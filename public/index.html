About me

Hi there, I’m Khang. If you want to find a professional video editor to help grow up your personal brand or make marketing videos to highlight yourself and your products, I'm the one you need.

I Specialize in editing talking head and faceless videos for social platforms, I also edit videos for websites, workshops, seminars, testimonials, events... to help you reach more viewers.

I have more than 3 years of experience in the industry. Starting my career as a freelancer, up to now I can be confident to let you know that I’m one of video editing experts.

My abilities have been proven on the upwork platform, one of the largest freelancer platforms globally.

Video editing is my passion

As a professional editor, I always put myself in the creator's perspective to edit videos at the highest quality. Quick turnaround time is one of the most important standards I provide clients. So, I always put all my energy into my work to bring satisfaction to clients.
